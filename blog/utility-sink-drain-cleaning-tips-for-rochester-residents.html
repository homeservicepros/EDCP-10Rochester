Common Causes of Utility Sink Clogs in Rochester Homes

Rochester residents, particularly in areas like Brighton and Maplewood, often encounter these utility sink issues:

  • Paint and chemical residue
  • Dirt and debris from gardening tools
  • Laundry lint and detergent buildup
  • Hair and soap scum
  • Mineral deposits from hard water

DIY Utility Sink Drain Cleaning Methods

1. Natural Cleaning Solution

Pour 1 cup of baking soda followed by 1 cup of white vinegar. Wait 15 minutes, then flush with hot water. This eco-friendly method is particularly effective for Rochester homes with sensitive plumbing systems.

2. Preventive Maintenance

Install a drain strainer to catch debris and clean it weekly. For more serious clogs, consider our professional sewer camera inspection service to identify deeper issues.

3. Hot Water Flush

Weekly hot water flushes help prevent grease and soap buildup, especially important during Rochester's cold winters.

When to Call Professional Drain Cleaners

While DIY methods work for minor clogs, some situations require professional intervention. If you notice these signs, it's time to call Rochester Emergency Drain Cleaning Pros:

  • Persistent slow draining
  • Foul odors
  • Multiple clogged drains
  • Gurgling sounds
