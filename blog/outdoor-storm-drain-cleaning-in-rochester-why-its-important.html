Why Storm Drain Cleaning Matters in Rochester

Rochester's climate, with its heavy spring rains and winter snow melt, puts significant pressure on our storm drainage systems. Properly maintained storm drains are essential for:

  • Preventing property flooding and water damage
  • Protecting foundation integrity
  • Maintaining proper yard drainage
  • Avoiding costly emergency repairs

Common Storm Drain Problems in Rochester

Our team regularly encounters these issues in Greece and Charlotte neighborhoods:

  • Leaf and debris accumulation
  • Ice blockages during winter
  • Root infiltration
  • Sediment buildup

Professional Storm Drain Cleaning Solutions

Our expert technicians utilize advanced techniques, including high-pressure drain jetting to effectively clear stubborn blockages. For persistent issues, we recommend our slow drain maintenance services.

Preventative Maintenance Tips

To maintain healthy storm drains, consider these essential steps:

  • Regular inspection of drain covers
  • Removal of visible debris
  • Professional cleaning twice yearly
  • Immediate attention to slow drainage

When to Call a Professional

Contact our experts immediately if you notice:

  • Standing water around drain areas
  • Unusual odors from drains
  • Visible damage to drain structures
  • Slow water drainage
