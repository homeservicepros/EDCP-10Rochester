Frequently Asked Questions

How often should outdoor storm drains be cleaned?

We recommend professional cleaning at least twice per year, typically in spring and fall, to prevent blockages and flooding. Properties with many trees may require more frequent service.

What are the signs that my storm drain needs cleaning?

Watch for standing water, slow drainage, unpleasant odors, or visible debris accumulation. These are indicators that professional cleaning is needed.
